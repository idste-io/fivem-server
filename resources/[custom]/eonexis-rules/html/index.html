Eonexis — Server Rules

§1 — Respect

  • Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • No trolling, griefing, or intentional disruption of others' roleplay.
  • Keep disputes in character — resolve OOC issues in Discord.

§2 — Roleplay Standards

  • Stay in character at all times unless in an OOC zone or channel.
  • No random death match (RDM) — you must have valid RP reason to attack.
  • No vehicle death match (VDM) — do not use vehicles as weapons without RP reason.
  • Value your life (VLT) — act as a real person would when threatened.
  • No metagaming — don't use out-of-character information in-game.
  • No powergaming — don't force actions on others without consent.

§3 — New Life Rule (NLR)

  • After dying, you forget everything that happened in that situation.
  • You may not return to the scene of your death for 15 minutes.

§4 — Cops / EMS

  • Do not kill emergency services unless they are a direct threat to your life.
  • A minimum of 2 cops must be online to rob a bank or major business.
  • Hostages must be treated fairly — no torture or execution without RP escalation.

§5 — Cheating & Exploits

  • Zero tolerance for hacks, mods, or exploits. Permanent ban.
  • Report bugs immediately to staff — do not abuse them.
  • Do not duplicate money, items, or vehicles by any means.

§6 — Community

  • Join our Discord for updates, support, and community events.
  • Staff decisions are final. Argue in tickets, not in-game.
  • Have fun and make great stories — that's what we're here for.
